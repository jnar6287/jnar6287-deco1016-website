It's a good idea to nominate who you'd like your super to be paid to if you die. You can make a non-binding nomination now, and a binding nomination once you've joined.

Apply for cover

Once you've joined you can apply for Death, Total & Permanent Disablement (TPD) and Income Protection cover anytime. You'll need to provide detailed health information to insure. Age limits and conditions apply.

You may also be able to transfer cover from another insurer.
